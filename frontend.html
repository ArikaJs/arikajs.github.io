Frontend & Ark Templates

Ark is the native template engine for ArikaJS. It is designed to be expressive, ultra-fast, and familiar to developers coming from Laravel or Django.

The Compilation Lifecycle

Unlike simple template engines that perform string replacement at runtime, Ark compiles your .ark.html files into optimized JavaScript functions. During development, this happens on-the-fly. In production, templates are pre-compiled and cached in the storage/framework/views directory, resulting in near-zero rendering overhead.

Layouts & Inheritance

Ark follows a powerful inheritance model. You can define a base layout with "slots" for content using the @layout and @section directives:

resources/views/layouts/main.ark.html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<body>
    <nav>...</nav>
    @yield('content')
    <footer>...</footer>
</body>
</html>

Advanced Directives

Ark supports a wide range of directives to handle logic cleanly inside your views:

  • @include('partial'): Renders a sub-view and passes existing data.
  • @asset('path'): Generates a versioned URL for your CSS and JS files to handle cache-busting.
  • @auth: Conditionals based on the user's authentication state.
