Agentic Development

ArikaJS is the world's first "agent-native" framework. It is designed to be easily read, modified, and maintained by both humans and AI agents.

The Agentic Vision

Modern development is increasingly collaborative between developers and AI. ArikaJS embraces this by providing a highly structured, predictable architecture that allows AI agents to understand the intent of your code and help you build faster.

Self-Healing Architecture

Through our advanced metadata and standardized directory structures, ArikaJS allows for "self-healing" systems. Use our Agentic CLI to auto-fix broken migrations or resolve dependency injection conflicts with a single command:

arika agent:fix

Standardized Prompts

We provide standardized prompts and schemas for LLMs (Large Language Models) so that your favorite AI tools can generate complete ArikaJS modules with 100% accuracy every time.
